# Ownership and Stewardship

## Ownership

The SMILE platform is a digital public good developed under the coordination of the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) in collaboration with the Ministry of Health of the Republic of Indonesia and technical partners.

Unless otherwise stated, all intellectual property rights for the SMILE source code and documentation are held by:

**United Nations Development Programme (UNDP)**

## Stewardship and Governance

- **Product Steward:** UNDP  
- **Government Counterpart:** Ministry of Health, Republic of Indonesia  
- **Technical Maintainer:** BADR (implementation and technical maintenance)

Following project closure, UNDP retains stewardship responsibility to ensure continuity, openness, and compliance with Digital Public Good principles. Technical maintenance may be delegated to designated partners under UNDP oversight.

## Contributions

External contributions are welcome and are governed by the license stated in the repository. Contributors retain copyright to their contributions while granting the project the rights defined in the license.
